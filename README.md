# [Capstone](https://capstone.jer.app)

Built in SF at [Hack Club](https://hackclub.com)'s [Horizons Nexus](https://nexus.hackclub.com) hackathon

A multiplayer puzzle platformer where players must use their magic abilities wisely, they're permanent. If they use them at the wrong time, they will have no choice but to restart.

## Development
* Godot 4.7 with Compatibility renderer
* Libresprite for 16px assets

## Gameplay and Theme Card Incorporation
* 2 player puzzle platformer, similar to games like Fire Boy and Water Girl.
* Player collision and momentum physics used to solve puzzles
* Theme: Magic - The players have magic abilities
* Theme: Permanent - The players' abilities and world interactions are permanent
* Theme: Call - The players have the ability to _call_ the other player to them (X, N), but they are permanently bound together
* Theme: Smol - The players have the ability to become _small_ permamently
* Additional game elements like switches, doors, and spikes add to the challenge
